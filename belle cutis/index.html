About Dr. Anuja Agarwal

With over two decades of dedicated practice in dermatology, Dr. Anuja Agarwal has established Belle Cutis with a vision to provide the highest quality skin care treatment using the latest advancements in dermatological science. Her approach combines medical expertise with personalized care, ensuring that each patient receives a tailored treatment plan for their unique skin concerns.

Dr. Anuja is committed to excellence, continuous education, and creating a warm, supportive environment where patients feel heard, valued, and confident in their treatment journey.
